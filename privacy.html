👻 Ghosting Check AI 🇬🇧 ENGLISH Privacy Policy – Last updated: January 1, 2026 The developer of Ghosting Check AI ("App") is committed to protecting user data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. 1. Data We Collect Account data: If you sign in with Google or Apple, your name and email address are stored via the Supabase authentication system. We do not store passwords. Chat messages: Messages you type in the app — if you are signed in — are stored in the Supabase cloud database to sync across devices. In guest mode, data is stored on-device only. Profile data: Your name, situation, and date of birth if you provide them — used only for personalized analysis. Subscription data: In-app purchases are managed by RevenueCat. We do not store card details. Notification data: Your daily check-in responses are stored on-device only (AsyncStorage). 2. How We Use Your Data Data is used solely to operate the App and provide personalized AI analysis. We never sell your data to third parties and do not use it for advertising. AI analysis runs through the Anthropic Claude API. Submitted text is also subject to Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy 3. Data Storage and Security Cloud-stored data resides on Supabase's secure servers (EU region) over encrypted connections. Local data is stored in the device's protected storage. 4. Data Retention You can delete your chat history at any time in the App (Settings → Delete Chat). To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. 5. Children's Privacy The App is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 17. 6. Third-Party Services The App uses the following external platforms: • Supabase – database and authentication: supabase.com/privacy • Anthropic Claude API – AI analysis: anthropic.com/privacy • RevenueCat – subscription management: revenuecat.com/privacy • Google Sign-In – optional login: policies.google.com/privacy • Apple Sign-In – optional login: apple.com/legal/privacy 7. Contact and Data Deletion For questions or data deletion requests, contact us: Email: anitaaicoursera@gmail.com We will fulfill data deletion requests within 30 days. 8. Changes We will notify users of policy changes in the next App update.
