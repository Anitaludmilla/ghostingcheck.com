Privacy Policy – Last updated: January 1, 2026

The developer of Ghosting Check AI is committed to protecting user data.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: Name and email via Supabase authentication. We do not store passwords.

Chat messages: Stored in Supabase cloud if signed in; on-device only in guest mode.

Profile data: Name, situation, date of birth – only for personalized analysis.

Subscription data: Managed by RevenueCat. We do not store card details.

Notification data: Daily check-in responses stored on-device only.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to operate the App and provide personalized AI analysis. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

AI analysis runs through the Anthropic Claude API: anthropic.com/privacy

3. Data Storage and Security

Cloud data is stored on Supabase secure servers (EU region) over encrypted connections.

4. Data Retention

Delete your chat history anytime in Settings → Delete Chat. Account deletion requests: email below.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is for users 17+. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17.
