Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Ghosting Check AI ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application developed by Baranyi Anita EV. We help users understand and heal from ghosting and relationship patterns using AI-powered analysis.

Contact: hello@ghostingcheck.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Email address — when you create an account (via email, Google, or Apple Sign In)
  • Conversation text and screenshots — that you voluntarily submit for analysis. These are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Usage data — number of analyses per day (for rate limiting), stored anonymously
  • Device ID — a random identifier stored locally on your device for guest usage tracking
  • Subscription status — managed through RevenueCat (whether you have a premium subscription)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-powered relationship analysis and coaching
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To enforce daily usage limits for free users
  • To send you opt-in push notifications (daily check-ins, morning messages)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Anthropic — AI analysis provider. Conversation content is processed by Anthropic's API. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — Authentication and database. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Google / Apple — Sign-in providers (optional).

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app's Profile screen — this permanently deletes all your data from our systems within 30 days.

Conversation texts submitted for analysis are not stored on our servers after the analysis is complete.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@ghostingcheck.com.
